Symbolism

The painting above is a painting named “The Fables” by Gustave Moreau, which show the mystical and dreamlike aspects of Symbolism.


Origin:

Symbolism, originating in the late 19th century as a reaction against naturalism, tried to convey abstract and emotional ideas through symbolic imagery.

Ideas & Characteristics:

Symbolism aimed to evoke emotions and convey deeper meanings by using symbols and metaphors, emphasizing the spiritual, mystical, and dreamlike aspects of art. It’s characteristics include symbolic imagery like allegories, symbols, etc. It is focused on creating a certain mood and atmosphere using colour, structure and symbolism. It emphasize the subjectivity of an author.

Artists:

Artists of Symbolism are such as Gustave Moreau or Odilon Redon.
